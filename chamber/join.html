NP Membership Benefits

Our Non-Profit Membership is designed specifically for registered charities and non-profit organizations operating in the Gweru area.

  • Basic listing in the Chamber directory
  • Access to Chamber networking events
  • Monthly newsletter subscription
  • Opportunity to collaborate on community projects
  • Promotion of your events on Chamber social media
×

Bronze Membership Benefits

Our Bronze Membership is perfect for small businesses and startups looking to establish their presence in the Gweru business community.

  • Enhanced listing in the Chamber directory
  • Access to all Chamber networking events
  • Monthly newsletter subscription
  • Social media mention (quarterly)
  • Access to business resources and workshops
  • Discounted rates for Chamber event spaces
×

Silver Membership Benefits

Our Silver Membership offers enhanced benefits for growing businesses looking to expand their network and visibility.

  • Premium listing in the Chamber directory
  • Access to all Chamber networking events
  • Monthly newsletter subscription with business spotlight opportunity
  • Social media promotion (monthly)
  • Access to exclusive business resources and workshops
  • 50% discount on Chamber event spaces
  • Participation in Chamber's business mentorship program
  • Ribbon cutting ceremony for new business or location
×

Gold Membership Benefits

Our Gold Membership provides premium benefits and maximum exposure for established businesses in the Gweru area.

  • Featured listing in the Chamber directory
  • VIP access to all Chamber events
  • Monthly newsletter subscription with dedicated business feature
  • Regular social media promotion (bi-weekly)
  • Access to all business resources and premium workshops
  • Free use of Chamber event spaces (up to 4 times per year)
  • Participation in Chamber's business mentorship program
  • Ribbon cutting ceremony for new business or location
  • Sponsorship recognition at major Chamber events
  • Representation in Chamber's government advocacy efforts
  • Annual business spotlight on Chamber website
