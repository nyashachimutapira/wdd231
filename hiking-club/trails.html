Popular Hiking Trails

Chimanimani Mountains

The Chimanimani Mountains offer stunning views and diverse wildlife. This trail features rocky terrain and beautiful waterfalls, making it a perfect choice for adventurous hikers.

Hwange National Park

Known for its large elephant population, Hwange National Park provides a unique hiking experience. The trails are well-marked, and hikers can enjoy spotting various wildlife along the way.

Matobo National Park

Matobo National Park is famous for its impressive rock formations and rich cultural heritage. Hikers can explore ancient rock art and enjoy breathtaking views from the hilltops.

Nyanga National Park

Nyanga National Park offers lush landscapes and scenic trails. Hikers can experience the beauty of the Mutarazi Falls and the highest peaks in Zimbabwe.
