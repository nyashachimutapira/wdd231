Essential Safety Tips for Hiking

Before heading out on your next adventure, keep these safety tips in mind:

  • Plan Ahead: Know your route and check the weather forecast.
  • Stay Hydrated: Carry enough water and drink regularly.
  • Wear Appropriate Gear: Use suitable footwear and clothing for the terrain and weather.
  • Tell Someone: Inform a friend or family member about your hiking plans.
  • Follow Trail Markers: Stay on marked trails to avoid getting lost.
  • Pack a First Aid Kit: Be prepared for minor injuries and emergencies.
  • Be Aware of Wildlife: Know how to react if you encounter wildlife.
