About Bishop Wilderness Explorers

At Bishop Wilderness Explorers, we are passionate about connecting people with nature through hiking and outdoor adventures. Our mission is to provide safe, enjoyable, and educational hiking experiences for everyone.

Our Mission

To inspire a love for the outdoors, promote environmental stewardship, and facilitate community through hiking.

Our Values

  • Community: We believe in building a supportive community of hiking enthusiasts.
  • Safety: We prioritize the safety of our members on all hikes.
  • Respect for Nature: We are committed to preserving the beauty of our natural environment.
  • Inclusivity: We welcome adventurers of all ages and skill levels.
